SRE controlled failure workflow

How SRE asks v2 to prove detection and recovery

1. RequestPlain-language lower-env failure request.
2. DraftChaosTest spec with scope, owner, aborts, signals.
3. ApproveHITL and GitOps review before mutation.
4. InjectLitmus/Argo runs bounded chaos.
5. TriageSpecialists gather K8s, Grafana, GitOps, KB evidence.
6. ScoreLifecycle eval checks detection, HITL, verification.
7. ReportGrafana, markdown, and review-manager follow-up.
