# Holiday Out-of-Office Messages

Use `[DATE]` for the first day of leave and `[RETURN_DATE]` where a return date is useful.

## Final Boarding Call

🚨 FINAL BOARDING CALL 🚨

This is your two-week warning: I am officially going on holiday as of [DATE] and I will not be contactable, findable, or emotionally available.

If you have ANYTHING you need from me - information, decisions, passwords, moral support, the thing I definitely said I'd send you - COME TO ME NOW. The window is closing.

I will be somewhere with a drink in hand, pretending Kubernetes doesn't exist. 🍹

Godspeed. 🫡

## Variants

### Polite But Firm

Two-week warning: I am going on holiday from [DATE].

If you need decisions, context, access, approvals, handovers, or the mysterious thing I promised to send you, please come to me now. Once I am off, I will be fully offline and happily unreachable.

After [DATE], all urgent matters should go through the agreed backup route. I will return on [RETURN_DATE].

### Slightly More Dramatic

🚨 TWO-WEEK WARNING 🚨

I am leaving the building, the chat, the incident channel, and possibly my sense of responsibility on [DATE].

If you need anything from me - decisions, passwords, explanations, handovers, reassurance, or that file I definitely said I had - now is the time. Do not wait until I am spiritually checked in at the airport.

After [DATE], I will be unavailable and pretending Slack is a myth.

### Kubernetes-Free Zone

Heads up: from [DATE], I will be on holiday and entering a strict Kubernetes-free zone.

Please send me anything you need before then: decisions, context, access requests, handover notes, or questions that currently live only in my head.

Once I am off, I will not be checking messages. I will be doing important strategic work involving sunshine, snacks, and not debugging clusters.

### Friendly Team Version

Quick reminder: I am on holiday from [DATE].

If there is anything you need from me before I go - project context, decisions, handover notes, access help, or a quick sanity check - please grab me this week.

I will be offline while away, so anything urgent during that time should go through the normal cover route.

### Maximum Chaos

🚨 THIS IS NOT A DRILL 🚨

I vanish on [DATE].

If you need information, approvals, passwords, diagrams, decisions, emotional support, or the ancient knowledge buried somewhere in a thread from three months ago, summon me now.

After [DATE], I will be unavailable, unhelpful, and extremely committed to not knowing what a cluster is.

May your alerts be quiet and your handovers be readable.

### Short Slack Version

Two-week warning: I am on holiday from [DATE] and will be fully offline.

If you need anything from me - decisions, access, context, handover notes, or that thing I owe you - please grab me now. After [DATE], I will be unavailable and pretending work does not exist.

### Manager-Safe Version

I will be on annual leave from [DATE] to [RETURN_DATE] and will be offline during that period.

Please send me anything you need before I go, especially decisions, handover items, access questions, or project context that may block work while I am away.

For urgent issues during my leave, please use the agreed cover process.
