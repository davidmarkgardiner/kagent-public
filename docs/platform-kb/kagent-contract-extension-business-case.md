# Business Case: Extend Embedded Engineer Contracts — kagent / Agentic Harness

Two embedded engineers solely own the kagent agentic platform, which automates
production remediation. No internal fallback exists. Losing them means lost
knowledge, an unowned safety-critical system, and a 6–12 month, two-engineer
backfill. Extending the contracts is the cheapest way to maintain continuity and
transfer knowledge.
